Workshops

Arduino

Our Arduino Workshop offered participants an engaging, hands-on experience with the Arduino platform. Attendees learned the fundamentals of electronics and microcontroller programming, explored sensors and actuators, and built their own functional prototypes. The workshop catered to beginners and enthusiasts alike, providing practical knowledge and skills in a collaborative environment. This event empowered participants to bring their ideas to life through interactive projects and creative problem-solving.

Open CV

The OpenCV Workshop introduced participants to the exciting world of computer vision. Attendees learned how to leverage OpenCV, a powerful library for image and video processing, to build applications that can analyze and manipulate visual data. Through hands-on exercises, participants gained practical experience in image recognition, object detection, and other core computer vision techniques. The workshop was designed for beginners and enthusiasts, fostering creativity and technical growth in a collaborative setting.

PCB Designing

In our PCB Designing Workshop, participants were introduced to the essential concepts of Printed Circuit Board (PCB) design. The workshop covered the fundamentals of PCB layout, component placement, and routing techniques using industry-standard software. Attendees gained hands-on experience in designing and simulating their own PCBs, equipping them with the skills to create efficient and reliable electronic circuits. This workshop was perfect for electronics enthusiasts looking to enhance their knowledge in hardware design.

ROS

Our ROS Workshop provided participants with an in-depth introduction to the Robot Operating System, a powerful framework for developing robotic applications. Attendees learned the fundamentals of ROS, including navigation, sensor integration, and robotic simulation using tools like Gazebo. The hands-on session helped participants build, program, and control robotic systems efficiently. This workshop was ideal for robotics enthusiasts and professionals looking to advance their skills in robotic development and automation.

CAD & 3D Printing

Our CAD & 3D Printing Workshop provided participants with a comprehensive introduction to computer-aided design (CAD) and the 3D printing process. Attendees learned how to create detailed 3D models using CAD software and explored the various techniques and technologies involved in 3D printing. The workshop featured hands-on sessions where participants designed and printed their own prototypes, fostering creativity and technical skills in a collaborative environment. This workshop was perfect for students and professionals looking to enhance their capabilities in design and manufacturing.

Raspberry Pi

Our Raspberry Pi Workshop introduced participants to the versatile world of Raspberry Pi, a popular microcomputer used for various projects and applications. Attendees learned how to set up their Raspberry Pi, explore its capabilities, and develop practical projects using Python and other programming languages. The hands-on sessions enabled participants to create their own projects, such as home automation systems, media centers, and IoT applications. This workshop was designed for beginners and enthusiasts alike, promoting creativity and technical skills in a supportive environment.
